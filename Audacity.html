Audacity

Audacity is another new software I learnt during this class. I have always wondered how people edit these podcasts with music of their choice, and now I found as well as learnt a great tool for it. I have always been into music, and singing, and I am really excited to record my songs and edit them in audacity.

It’s been a while since I visited India, so I decided to recreate some of my memories with different kinds of sounds. My uncle’s house is in a small town, and I have a lot of amazing memories there. Since I grew up in an industrial town, I really enjoyed the quiet moments there. It was a perfect spot for relaxation, since it did not have the hustle of the city.

My music track consists of sounds which could be related to that place, like the fresh morning sounds, the trains that passed the town once a while, the sound of rivers, bicycles,ice cream trucks, as well as the winds at night, and cricket chirping. I used most of the sounds from here, like the sound of waters, the sounds of trains, bicycle ( a friend of mine owns one), the sounds of pigeons in the morning( a lot of pigeons come in our balcony and have nests- we never disturb them), and the sounds at night time.
